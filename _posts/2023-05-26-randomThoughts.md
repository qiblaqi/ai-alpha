Hearing -> non-voice, listening; (listening to music, listening to the sound of nature, listening to the sound of the city)

My thoughts are generated by the input of my senses. For example, I see the world through my eyes, the people around me, nature, the view of the sunset, and the book I bought, which contains a lot of words. I hear the world through my ears: the voices of people, the sounds of nature, the sounds of the city, and the music I listen to.

I think the world is a place where we can get input from our senses, and we can generate thoughts from that input. The inner voice is a language that I can understand, and I can use it to communicate with myself. I think the inner voice is a language that I can use to communicate with other people. I think the inner voice is a language that I can use to communicate with the world.
